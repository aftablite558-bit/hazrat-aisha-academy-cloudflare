# PUBLIC RELEASE NOTES — v1.0.0
**Hazrat Aisha Academy — Official Digital School Platform**

## 🚀 Overview
Hazrat Aisha Academy is a flagship digital education platform serving students, parents, faculty, and the public community in Dilawarbagh, Sharif Colony, Chak Rajopatti, Sitamarhi, Bihar. This release completes **Phase 4 (Final Public Release)**, bringing a production-ready, highly responsive public portal integrated seamlessly with the administrative core.

---

## ✨ Key Capabilities & Highlights

### 1. Live Public Homepage
- **Dynamic Hero Banner**: Complete with Qur'anic du'a banner (*Rabbi Zidni 'Ilma*), action CTAs (*Apply for Admission*, *Learn More*), and quick navigation.
- **Real-Time Data Feeds**:
  - **Latest Notices & Circulars**: Direct integration with published database announcements.
  - **Life at Hazrat Aisha (Gallery)**: Live photo album preview cards.
  - **Achievements Section**: Spotlighting academic and co-curricular milestones.
  - **Parent Testimonials**: Real-time community feedback with rating indicators.
  - **Facilities & Features**: Detailed breakdown of campus infrastructure and Islamic values integration.

### 2. Interactive Public Lookups & Search
- **Attendance Portal**: Live search by Student Name, Class, and Roll Number with percentage calculation, history, working days counter, and detailed statuses (Present, Absent, Late, Leave).
- **Homework Portal**: Filtered class-wise homework list with downloadable attachments, creation timestamps, and teacher instructions.
- **Exam Schedule Portal**: Complete date sheet per subject with exam timings, pass marks, total marks, and venue.
- **Results Portal**: Searchable report card view with grade breakdowns, total percentages, status badges, and printable PDF capability.

### 3. Public Interactive Services
- **Online Admissions**: Digital application form with guardian details, document uploads, and instant submission tracking for administrative approval.
- **Careers & Vacancies**: Live published job openings, role requirements, and online application submission with resume file uploads.
- **Contact & Google Maps Location**: Embedded interactive map, direct WhatsApp / call connection (`+91 9470818538`), office hours, and message dispatch sending enquiries directly into the Admin Enquiries desk.
- **Public Feedback System**: Ticketed feedback and complaint logging page generating unique tracking IDs for community governance.
- **Photo Gallery & Lightbox**: Fullscreen modal image viewer with prev/next controls, keyboard navigation (arrow keys, ESC), zoom capabilities, and captions.
- **Document Downloads**: Public repository for syllabus, fee structures, circulars, and forms.

### 4. SEO & Technical Standards
- **Canonical URLs & Open Graph Tags**: Fully structured social share cards for Facebook, Twitter/X, and WhatsApp.
- **Indexed Search Endpoints**: Standard compliant `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`.
- **Performance & Lazy Loading**: Route-level React code splitting using `React.lazy` and `Suspense` for instantaneous initial page loads.
- **Accessibility & Contrast**: Accessible contrast ratios, explicit touch targets (44px+ on mobile), screen reader tags, and keyboard focus states.
